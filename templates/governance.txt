Governance Responsibilities – Ngā Haepapa Whakahaere

Purpose:
This policy supports our governing body to carry out its responsibilities in a way that is legally sound, transparent, and aligned with our kaupapa.

Roles and Responsibilities:
- Board/Trustees hold ultimate accountability
- Clear decision-making and delegation
- Regular reviews of strategy, risk, and resources

Foundations:
- Tika, pono, and mana-enhancing leadership
- Collective accountability
- Legal compliance with our governing documents

Commitments:
- We meet regularly and record our decisions
- We understand and respect our roles
- We uphold the mana of our rōpū through good governance

Footer:
This policy is reviewed regularly to remain tika, pono, and mana-enhancing.
